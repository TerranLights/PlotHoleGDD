[![Platform](https://img.shields.io/badge/Platform-PC-blue.svg)](https://godotengine.org)

> **You are a robot waiter on an endless train that definitely isn't glitching.**

A light-hearted, humorous first-person adventure where you play as a service droid trying to complete increasingly absurd fetch quests for eccentric passengers aboard a mysterious locomotive that may or may not be falling apart at the seams.

Full of obvious nods to Bethesda games.

![Banner Image](https://via.placeholder.com/1280x400/4a2c5a/ffffff?text=Plot+Hole+-+Robot+Waiter+Life)

## ✨ Features

- **Literal Fetch Quests** — "Bring me a glass of water" turns into a 20-minute odyssey through multiple train cars
- **Bethesda-Style Humor** — Glitchy NPCs, broken dialogue trees, and fourth-wall winks
- **Absurd Passengers** — A suspicious spy, a retired space pirate, a sentient suitcase, and that one guy who’s always in the dining car
- **Train Exploration** — Procedurally shifting cars, secret compartments, and the occasional car that’s just... wrong
- **Upgradable Robot Body** — Better legs for running, extra arms for carrying more trays, sarcasm module
- **Light-hearted & Family Friendly** — Pure comedic chaos with zero stress

## 🎮 Gameplay

Real-time 3D first-person. Walk (or roll) the endless train, serve drinks, fix broken quests, and slowly unravel the meta mystery of why this train exists.
